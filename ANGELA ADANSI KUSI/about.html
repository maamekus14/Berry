Welcome to Angie's BabyCare Center

Angie's BabyCare has been licensed by the Ministry of Education since 2018, providing high quality child care to it's community. Our educated staff is committed to providing high quality child care and education. It is the commitment and longevity of our teaching staff that allow us to have lasting relationships with families in our community


Our location in the community offers us additional resources. Walking trips to the library or a walk around the neighbourhood to explore the seasonal changes enhance our year round educational program

At Angie's BabyCare, we care about the whole child. From a well developed educational program, to meals that are prepared on site from fresh ingredients, it is our goal to help grow well rounded, healthy children. From educating children, to supporting the whole family, Angie's BabyCare is here to help Give Children the Roots to Grow, and the Wings to Fly!
